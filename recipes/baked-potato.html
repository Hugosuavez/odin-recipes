Baked Potato

Description

Jacket potato smashed out in the oven in an hour with very little effort

Ingredients

  • 1 potato
  • butter
  • salt and pepper
  • olive oil

Method

  1. preheat oven to correct temperature
  2. Rub olive oil over potato
  4. season with salt and pepper
  5. Put in oven for 1 hour
  6. butter to serve
