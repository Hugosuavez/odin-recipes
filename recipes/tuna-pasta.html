Tuna Pasta

Description

A quick and easy no fuss affordable weeknight classic.

Tuna, peas, spaghetti and a few basic ingredients to fill the hole

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna
  • Handful of frozen peas
  • Spaghetti
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • one knob of butter

Method

  1. Bring water to boil in pan, add pasta for 10 mins
  2. While pasta is cooking, add butter and peas to frying pan and gently simmer for 3 minutes
  3. Add garlic and tuna to the butter and peas
  4. Drain pasta and stir in with the rest of the ingredients
